Based in Carlton VIC, RMIT tasked students with re-designing the educational precinct located along Earl Street. "Burst" is designed to be a multi purpose space and study area which offers an alternative study environment to the main precinct along Swanston street.

Jones House

In the built up suburb of Cremorne, the Jones family wanted to build on a narrow site along White street. The design response optimises the use of the space, creates a harmony with its location, and is a modern take on classic forms.

Dights Falls Gallery & Cafe

The gallery at Dights falls aims to be harmonious with the land it is built on, without demanding too much attention. Split up into two structures, the gallery and the cafe, the structures have a consistent design language that creates a delicate backdrop to the natural landscape.
